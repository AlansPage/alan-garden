# Apparatus of Capture

The apparatus of capture is the mechanism by which the smooth becomes striated. By which the nomad is stopped. By which the flow is dammed and turned into property.

Deleuze and Guattari develop this in *A Thousand Plateaus*. Capital is an apparatus of capture. So is the State. So, in subtle ways, is language itself. They take what flows—labor, desire, thought—and convert it into measurable, ownable quantities.

[[Anti-Sisyphus]] describes the wanderer who refuses the rock. The apparatus of capture is the force that would hand him the rock. That would say: your movement is worthless until it produces something. Until it accumulates. Until it *returns*.

[[Nomadic-consciousness|Nomadic Consciousness]] resists capture by staying in motion. But capture is clever. It does not only block; it redirects. It says: your wandering is fine, but wander *here*. In this marketplace. In this platform. In this genre. The [[deterritorialization]] of the nomad is immediately followed by reterritorialization. Escape is always temporary.

I think about this in the context of [[desire-machines|desire]]. Desire flows. It connects. It does not want to be contained. But we live in a world of containers. Jobs contain labor. Relationships contain affect. Notes contain thought. The containers are not inherently bad—they make life possible. But they are apparatuses of capture. They turn flow into stock.

The [[rhizome]] evades capture because it has no center to seize. The [[body-without-organs]] evades capture because it refuses the organization that would make it legible to power. These are not solutions so much as directions. Ways of moving that make capture harder.

The question is not how to escape capture entirely—perhaps that is impossible. The question is how to stay porous. How to let the dams leak. How to remember that the stock was once flow, and could be again.
